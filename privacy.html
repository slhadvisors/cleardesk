1. Who we are

ClearDesk ("ClearDesk", "we", "us") provides software that helps accounting and tax firms automate client outreach — document chasing, deadline reminders and sales outreach — across AI voice calls, WhatsApp and SMS. This policy explains how we handle personal data when you use our website, dashboard and APIs (the "Service").

The data controller for account and website data is [ClearDesk legal entity name], [registered address], [country]. Questions: privacy@cleardesk.app.

2. Controller and processor roles

It matters in what capacity we handle data:

  • As a processor. When your firm uploads its clients' contact details and we place calls or send messages on your behalf, your firm is the controller of that client data and ClearDesk acts as a processor following your instructions and our Data Processing Addendum (DPA).
  • As a controller. For your own account data (firm details, user logins, billing, website usage), ClearDesk is the controller.

3. Data we collect

Account & firm data

Name, work email, phone, password (hashed), firm name, country/jurisdiction, role, profile photo, billing and credit-balance information.

Client contact data (uploaded by your firm)

Names, phone numbers, email addresses, filing status and any notes your firm imports or syncs from its CRM to run outreach.

Communications & activity data

Call metadata (time, duration, outcome), call recordings and transcripts where enabled, SMS/WhatsApp message content and delivery status, campaign configuration and AI scripts.

Technical data

IP address, device/browser type, session and authentication tokens, and product usage events used to operate and secure the Service.

4. How we use data

  • To provide the Service — authenticate users, run campaigns, place calls and send messages, and display logs and analytics.
  • To enforce credit limits, budget caps and the cost circuit-breaker, and to bill for usage.
  • To secure the platform — tenant isolation, fraud and abuse prevention, audit logging.
  • To provide support and send service notices.
  • To improve reliability and features (using aggregated or de-identified data where feasible).

We do not sell personal data, and we do not use your clients' contact data to train general-purpose AI models.

5. Legal bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Where GDPR applies we rely on: performance of a contract (providing the Service), legitimate interests (securing and improving the Service), legal obligation (tax, accounting, lawful requests), and consent where required. For your firm's outreach to its clients, your firm is responsible for establishing the lawful basis and obtaining any required consent (see the Terms and §10).

7. International transfers

ClearDesk serves firms in India, the US and the UAE, so data may be processed outside your country. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms for cross-border transfers.

8. Data retention

  • Standard accounts: client outreach records are retained for 7 days, after which they are overwritten with compliance placeholders.
  • Sandbox / guest trials: records are destroyed 2 hours after creation.
  • Account, billing and audit data is retained as long as your account is active and as required by law.

9. Security

We apply row-level security for strict per-firm tenant isolation, encryption in transit, role-based access control, scoped service credentials, and audit logging. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data and to notify affected parties of incidents as required by law.

10. Your rights

Subject to your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict or port your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these, contact privacy@cleardesk.app. If you are an individual whose data was uploaded by an accounting firm, please contact that firm (the controller); we will assist them in responding.

11. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication and session security, and limited analytics to operate the Service. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser or our cookie settings where offered.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Service evolves. Material changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, notified in-product or by email.
