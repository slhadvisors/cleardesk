Help & Support

Everything you need to get your firm running outreach on ClearDesk — and how to reach a human when you need one.

Quick start

Most firms are sending their first reminders within 15 minutes:

  1. Set up your workspace — confirm your firm name, jurisdiction (IN / US / AE) and default language in Settings.
  2. Connect a channel — link Twilio for voice & SMS, and WhatsApp Business for messaging, under Settings → Integrations.
  3. Import contacts — upload a CSV or sync your CRM segment from Contacts.
  4. Create a campaign — pick a purpose (document chaser, deadline reminder, sales outreach), review the AI script, choose a tone and channel, then run it manually or set it to automated.
  5. Track results — watch calls and messages land in Calls and SMS, with outcomes on your dashboard.

Frequently asked questions

Why can't I place calls yet?⌄
Voice and SMS require a connected Twilio account with a purchased number. Go to Settings → Integrations and connect Twilio. On a Twilio trial, recipients must be verified and some destinations (e.g. SMS to India) require an upgraded account with DLT registration.
How does ClearDesk keep each firm's data separate?⌄
Every record is scoped to your organization and enforced with database row-level security, so one firm can never see another's contacts, calls or messages. Access within your firm is controlled by roles.
How long is my data kept?⌄
Standard accounts retain outreach records for 7 days, after which they're overwritten with compliance placeholders. Sandbox/trial records are destroyed 2 hours after creation. See the Privacy Policy.
What happens when my credits run out?⌄
If overdraft is enabled you get a small buffer; otherwise the automated cost circuit-breaker pauses processing campaigns so you're never billed past your limit. Top up anytime under Settings → Billing.
Can I edit the AI script and voice?⌄
Yes. Scripts are generated but fully editable per campaign, with variables like {{first_name}} filled at send time. AI agents' voice, language and persona are configured under Agents.
Who is responsible for consent and do-not-call rules?⌄
Your firm is. You control the contacts you upload and are responsible for lawful basis, consent, opt-outs and permitted contact hours under local rules (TCPA, TRAI/DLT, UAE TDRA, WhatsApp policies, etc.). See the Terms.
Which countries and languages are supported?⌄
ClearDesk supports firms in India, the United States and the UAE, with localized telephony, AI voices and compliance rules per jurisdiction, and English, Hindi and Arabic interface options.
