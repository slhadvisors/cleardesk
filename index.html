Support

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Most firms launch their first live campaign within 20–30 minutes of signing up. Upload your contact list, choose a campaign type, review the AI-generated script, and hit Execute. ClearDesk handles the rest — routing calls through local carriers and retrying non-responders automatically.
No — ClearDesk integrates with your existing CRM (Zoho, HubSpot, Salesforce). It reads contact data and lead status from your CRM and writes call outcomes back. Think of it as a specialist outreach layer on top of your existing workflow, not a replacement for it.
ClearDesk uses premium voice synthesis via Vapi and ElevenLabs, producing very natural-sounding calls. We recommend being transparent with clients — stating it's an automated call from your firm at the start. You can customise the intro script and voice persona to match your brand tone and regional language.
ClearDesk uses multi-channel smart retry: if a voice call goes unanswered, it automatically escalates to a WhatsApp message, then an SMS. You control the cadence — minimum 4 hours between attempts — and can set a maximum retry count per contact. All attempts are logged in your campaign dashboard.
Yes. ClearDesk uses Supabase (PostgreSQL with Row-Level Security) ensuring your organisation's data is strictly isolated. All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.3. Standard client records are retained for 7 days after campaign completion, then purged. You can request immediate deletion at any time via your settings panel.
ClearDesk currently supports India (English, Hindi, Tamil), United States (English), and UAE (English, Arabic). Each jurisdiction uses locally-approved carriers to ensure deliverability — Exotel/Twilio in India, Twilio 10DLC in the US, and Du/Etisalat routing in the UAE. Additional languages can be requested on Enterprise plans.
